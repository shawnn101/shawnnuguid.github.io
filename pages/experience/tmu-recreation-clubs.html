Recreational and Intramural Lead Administrator | Current

What I Do & Use

In my Recreational and Intramural Lead Administrator role, I support intramural sports and recreation club operations while applying the technical workflows I built in other roles to everyday program needs. I help manage administrative processes, keep information organized, support hiring and applicant review workflows, and update website content so students and staff can find accurate program details.

This role is less about one single tool and more about connecting operations, documentation, communication, and systems thinking. I use Excel, website editing, applicant tracking workflows, scheduling, and staff coordination to make recreation club and intramural processes easier to run. It has helped me understand that a technical solution only matters if the people using it can trust it, understand it, and fit it into their workflow.
