TMU Intramural & Sport

Intramural Tech and Program Lead | May 2025 - May 2026

What I Did & Used

At TMU Intramural & Sport, I worked at the intersection of program operations and technical tooling. I built an ATS-style resume screening workflow in Python using Pandas and scikit-learn so applicants could be ranked more consistently against role requirements, relevant experience, and keyword evidence. That workflow helped reduce manual screening time and gave staff a clearer starting point for applicant review.

I also cleaned and maintained a database of more than 16,000 users, using Python and Excel to remove outdated records and improve data accuracy. On the reporting side, I built Tableau dashboards to help visualize participation trends, program activity, and operational decisions. The role helped me develop stronger judgment around building tools that are useful for non-technical staff, not just technically interesting.
