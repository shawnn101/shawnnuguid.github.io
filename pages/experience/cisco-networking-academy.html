Cisco

Sales & IoT Engineer, Cisco High School STEM Program | Jul 2022

What I Did & Learned

Through the Cisco High School STEM Program, I joined a virtual summer co-op that introduced students to Cisco's technology, culture, and engineering environment. I shadowed Cisco employees, worked with a team under professional mentors, and attended IoT sessions, project work, executive speaker events, and development activities that gave me a clearer view of how large technical organizations operate.

One of the biggest parts of the program was the capstone project with my team, Team Nebula. I created and pitched the original idea that helped shape the final product, then worked with the group as that idea turned into a finished presentation. The role exposed me to IoT, engineering, sales, mentorship, and team-based product thinking, while also helping me build confidence in presenting technical ideas and working with people outside my usual school environment.
